I was trying to figure out how to sort, combine, and limit the number of items in some existing RSS feeds I manage. Enter Yahoo Pipes.

Pipes lets you do some powerful things with RSS feeds and other web content.

Check out my first three pipes:

  • http://pipes.yahoo.com/billglick/gracelctvpodcast
    combines LifeChurch.tv and FindingGrace podcasts
  • http://pipes.yahoo.com/billglick/digdeep
    limits the feed down to only 1 item
  • http://pipes.yahoo.com/billglick/newsalerts
    search Yahoo and Google news and display unique alerts

This seems very powerful. I want to see about creating some other custom feeds from various sources.
